What is it?

Bigby Wolf is a full-featured WhatsApp bot based on a character I'm really fan of, which is called Bigby Wolf from the comics “Fables”. (Oh and I strongly recommend you to play the game, it's amazing!).

Do I have to pay for it?

No! It's completely free and there are no limits. But you can help me keep it running by donating any amount.

Some of the available features

Command What does it do?
!help Sends a list of all available features.
!sticker Turns any image/video or gif into a sticker!
!image Google image search.
!search Google search.
!lyrics Finds the lyrics for any song.
!mp3 Downloads any song from YouTube.
!mp4 YouTube video downloader.
!ris Reverse image search.
