Hi there! I'm Luan, a space nerd, programmer, and hobbyist astrophotographer from Brazil. Observing the cosmos and learning more about it are among my passions; the vastness of it all and the mysteries that lie within kindle my curiosity.

As you may have noticed, I like computers too. I've been coding since I was a kid; the first programming language I learned was Java. Some of my earliest complex applications were Android games (using LibGDX) and a couple of GB, GBA, and Chip8 emulators (I haven't worked on similar projects in a while, but emulators are still my favorite type of software). Nowadays, I mostly work on web applications and reverse engineering projects.

And last but not least, I love reading! Feel free to send me book recommendations, I'm always looking for new things to read.
