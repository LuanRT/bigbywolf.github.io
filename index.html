Bigby Wolf - Project

Bigby Wolf is a full-featured WhatsApp bot, it's based on a character I'm really fan of, which is called Bigby Wolf from the comics “Fables”. (Oh and I strongly recommend you to play the game, it's amazing!).

To use it go to this number and type !help.

Commands

Just a little snippet of the available features, you can check all of them by sending !help to the bot.

!help • sends a list of all available features.
!sticker • converts any image/video or gif into a sticker!
!image term • google image search.
!search term • google search.
!ris • reverse image search.
!mp4 term • YouTube video downloader.
!mp3 term • downloads any song from YouTube.
!lyrics song-name • finds the lyrics for any song.

And much more...
